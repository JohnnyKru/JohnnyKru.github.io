Johnny Keokongsy

Hometown: Greensboro, NC.

About Johnny: Started his training at age 5.

He was taught by his Father, a former fighter from Camp Mung Si Koat located between Laos and Thailand.

Instructor Johnny Keokongsy has also studied other martial arts such as Tae Kwon Do and Brazilian Jiu jitsu.

He has five plus years of teaching experience and would like to continue to not only perfect, but also share his craft with others.

He has made it his mission to preserve the traditional sport of Muay Thai as well as carry on the teachings of where it came from Thailand.
